Top 5 Things to Do in the Fall in Colorado, 2022

Plans List


Red Rocks Amphitheatre

The only naturally-occurring, acoustically perfect amphitheatre in the world. Located in Morrison.


The Flatirons

Slanted, sandstone rock formations that are an iconic part of the Rocky Mountains. Located in Boulder.


Hanging Lake

Perched high in the mountains, the trail takes you along Dead Horse Creek and through the rocky Glenwood Canyon. It is a national natural landmark located Glenwood Springs.


Garden of the Gods

Known for its massive red rocks sticking straight out of the ground, tourists come from all over the world to wander the trails, see the wildlife and witness the formations for themselves, located in Colorado Springs.


Denver

Its elevation 5,280 feet, gives the city it's iconic nickname “Mile High City,” and a mild, sunny, dry climate are distinctive characteristics.
